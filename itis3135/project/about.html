Our Story

Chopbox is a family-owned Vietnamese and Japanese fusion restaurant serving fresh and traditional recipes made with love. Located in downtown Concord, we bring together bold flavors and a casual dining experience for the local community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options? ▾

Yes, we offer a variety of plant-based dishes. Ask our staff for recommendations.

Is parking available? ▾

Yes, there is street parking and parking decks nearby.

Can I place an order online? ▾

Yes, you can order through DoorDash or Uber Eats for delivery and order pick oneline here.

Do you take reservations? ▾

We currently operate on a walk-in basis only. However, you can contact us for parties and celebrations.

Are your dishes made fresh? ▾

Yes, all our dishes are made fresh to order using locally sourced ingredients.

Do you have gluten-free options? ▾

We offer several gluten-free options. Please ask our staff for guidance when ordering.

Is the restaurant kid-friendly? ▾

Yes, we have high chairs available and our menu includes kid-friendly options.
