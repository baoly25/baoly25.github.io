Boundless Lynx Park is a unique zoo dedicated entirely to felines, showcasing cats of all sizes, from cute domestic breeds like British Shorthairs to wild predators like lions and of course lynxes. Visitors can explore exhibits featuring leopards, cheetahs, sphynxes, and a variety of other wild and domestic felines. Visitors can interact with the domestic cats in a dedicated petting area where they can play with friendly breeds like the Scottish Folds. This hands-on experience allows guests to connect with these cats up close while learning about their behaviors, care, and unique characteristics. Educational signs will be displayed throughout as visitors approach the animals and learn more about them. The park highlights the diversity of the feline family, giving guests a chance to appreciate their unique behaviors, adaptations, and roles in nature.
