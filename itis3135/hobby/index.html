About Me

Prompt: "Manga style depiction of Zeri from League of Legends."

I got into League of Legends in 2022 which was very late considering the game had been over 10 years old by then. I usually enjoy shooter games such as Valorant, Apex Legends, Fortnite, and Overwatch. Never would I have thought I would ever play League of Legends since the only time I tried it, I hated it. This all changed in late 2022 when I decided to watch League of Legends Worlds 2022 which had a lot of coverage during that time. This was when I discovered the team DRX and a player named Deft. I enjoyed their underdog story and was inspired to play. From this point on I started to play League of Legends in the role of ADC like my favorite player Deft.

Best Times

You can play League of Legends any time of the day. Pick a time that is right for you and hop into the rift! My favorite time to play games is when I am finished with all my responsibilities for that day. As a result, my playing time is usually later in the day. I also enjoy watching League of Legends Esports which runs throughout most of the year.

Best Time to Watch Esports

  • Spring & Summer Splits: Regional leagues (LCK, LPL, LEC, LTA) run from January to August.
  • Mid-Season Invitational (MSI): International showdown in May.
  • World Championship (Worlds): The biggest event, held in October - November.
