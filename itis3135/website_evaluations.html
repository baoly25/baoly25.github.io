Website Evaluations

Good Website - Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley official website offers a simple and clean aesthetic that matches the game's art style. It showcases features of the game, purchase options, graphics, socials, and additional informations like the forum, wiki, and merch.

  • Contrast: Uses light background color with dark text that are easy to read. The green of the game's logo stand out from the blue skies which creates balance and a nice aesthetic.
  • Repetition: Font style and colors are consistent throughout the website and also matches with the looks of the game.
  • Alignment: Everything is neatly aligned with clear sections. Headings, images, and buttons follow a structured layout.
  • Proximity: Content is grouped together correctly and spacing is well balanced. Content is centered with a clear margin on the left and right.

Bad Website - MIT Virtual Tour

The MIT Virtual Tour website lacks design, outdated, and overall very dull. It looks like what would happen when you turn off CSS on a website. This website offer a virtual tour of the campus however, it only provides only low quality images. This feel like a forgotten website or it is intentionally like this.

  • Contrast: Uses a white background with black text and blue links for most of the content. Buttons for the virtual tour can be difficult to navigate as they are blurry and contrast is not clear.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent font and different layout between some pages.
  • Alignment: Elements are not properly aligned, with text and images sometimes appearing misplaced. Lacks a clear grid structure to the page.
  • Proximity: Content is poorly placed with the nav bar being at the bottom. Spacing is uneven with some places having too much and others too little. Overall, the flow of the content does not logically flow which might confuse users.
